## Key Insights

1. **Pragmatic Formalization**: TypeScript occupies a unique epistemological position between strict formalism (Haskell/Coq) and pure dynamism (Python/JS).
2. **The Art of Trade-offs**: The JS/TS stack succeeds not through any single optimal technology, but through elegant balancing of multiple constraints.
3. **Runtime Convergence**: Node.js, Bun, and Deno are driving collective ecosystem evolution through competition, not zero-sum replacement.
